Halloumi Burger

The Tastiest Halloumi Burger Ever

Are you looking for the the best Halloumi Burger recipe? Well, we've got you covered!

This recipe is simple - yet so freaking tasty! Give it a try, I promise that you won't be dissapointed.

Ingredients

  • 200 g halloumi
  • hamburger Buns
  • 3 tbsp hummus
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • sliced tomato

Steps

  1. Brush each side of all the halloumi slices with olive oil and cook them on each side for 2-3 mins or until golden.
  2. Split the brioche buns (toast them very briefly on both sides if you like, but be careful not to burn them) and spread the bottom halves with the hummus. Add some tomato and then the halloumi, then finish with the lettuce on top with the other half of the bun. Enjoy.
